Multiple Charts

Multiple chart types over a given domain. For example: 

    <bc-d3-polymer-singraph
      height="{{height}}"
      data="{{data}}"
      domain="{{domain}}"
      width="{{width}}"
      debug="{{debug}}">
    </bc-d3-polymer-singraph>

    <bc-d3-polymer-bargraph
      height="{{height}}"
      data="{{data}}"
      domain="{{domain}}"
      width="{{width}}"
      debug="{{debug}}">
    </bc-d3-polymer-bargraph>
